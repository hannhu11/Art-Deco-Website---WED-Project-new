Cultural Impact

Art Deco in cinema, music and popular culture

Cinema & Entertainment

The Great Gatsby (2013)

Baz Luhrmann perfectly recreated the Art Deco aesthetics of the 1920s, from costume design to set decoration.

Blade Runner (1982, 2049)

Ridley Scott combined Art Deco with cyberpunk, creating a unique visual style for the future city.

Bioshock (2007)

The underwater city Rapture was designed entirely in Art Deco style, creating a unique atmosphere.

Music & Performance

Lady Gaga

Many of Lady Gaga's MVs and stage designs draw inspiration from Art Deco with geometric visuals and luxury aesthetic.

Broadway Shows

Many productions like "Chicago", "Cabaret" use Art Deco in stage design and costume.

Concert Stages

Artists like Beyoncé, Taylor Swift have used Art Deco elements in stage design for tour concerts.

Art Deco In The Digital Age

Web Design

Typography & Layout

Sans-serif fonts with geometric forms, golden ratio layouts, vertical emphasis in navigation design.

Color Schemes

Black-gold-white, metallics, high contrast combinations are favored in luxury websites.

UI Elements

Buttons with geometric borders, zigzag dividers, sunburst patterns in icons.

Mobile Apps

Luxury Brands

Cartier, Tiffany & Co., Chanel apps use Art Deco elements to reinforce brand luxury.

Finance Apps

Banking and investment apps apply Art Deco to create trust and sophistication.

Gaming

Casino games, puzzle games with Art Deco theming are increasingly popular.

AI & Digital Art

Midjourney & DALL-E

AI art generators are trained to create art pieces in Art Deco style with high accuracy.

NFT Collections

Many successful NFT collections use Art Deco aesthetic to attract collectors.

Virtual Reality

VR experiences recreate 1920s environments with Art Deco architecture.
